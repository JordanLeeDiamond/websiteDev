The AE86 Trueno.
Manufactured from 1984-1987 this vehicle had such a short manufacturing run but such a huge impact.
This vehicle was the star of the anime ‘Initial D’.
Driven by the main protagonist Takumi, this anime introduced drifting and JDM culture to millions around the world.
To this day there are still people discovering Initial D and finding a newfound love for the JDM culture.
The AE86 is a variant of the Toyota Corolla, it is. Small front-engine / rear-wheel-drive vehicle.
Very much like the Civic this car was obtainable and being able to drive a car that a hero of your favorite anime drove,
give’s you the feeling that you could drive flat out down Mount Akina drifting around the blind turns
in the middle of the night whilst you are racing much more powerful machines and somehow winning.
