The Honda Civic Type R.
Honda Type R models are special performance editions of their base model vehicles.
The design of Type R models was originally focused on race conditions,
with an emphasis on minimizing weight, and maximizing performance potential.
The Honda Civic is a car that undoubtedly introduced thousands of people to the JDM culture.
One of the main reasons for this would have to be the affordability of a Honda Civic compared to other JDM cars.
Along with the massive aftermarket for upgrades that can be made to this vehicle and the huge following that this particular vehicle has,
you will always have something to do and someone to talk to.
