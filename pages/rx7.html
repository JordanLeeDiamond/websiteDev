The Mazda RX7 or the Mazda Rotary Experiment #7.
The RX7 was manufactured between 1992 - 2002.
The USP that the RX7 has over all the other JDM legends is that it is fitted with a Rotary Engine.
Along with the innovative Rotary Engine,
this generation of the RX7 was fitted with a very technically advanced Sequential Twin Turbo Charger.
This combination allows this machine to make 238bhp and a 0-60 time of 5.2 seconds.
You cannot think about the RX7 without also thinking about a Veilside body kit.
The Veilside RX7 is arguably more iconic than the base Mosel RX7 FD.
It transforms the car to look unrecognizable and something that should have been released in 2021, not 1992.
