The Nissan Skyline R34 GTR or better known as Godzilla.
This engineering masterpiece was originally manufactured from 1998 - 2002.
A technologically advanced display unit set the model apart from other JDM cars,
while its RB26 twin-turbo engine produced an impressive 320 horsepower.
The body was also stiffer, the aerodynamics were improved, and several weight-saving measures were employed,
including the use of a carbon fiber rear diffuser.
Most notably the R34 GTR appeared in several of the “Fast and Furious” movies.
However, none were as impactful as its appearance in 2 Fast 2 Furious.
Watching Paul Walker's character Brian race through the streets of Miami in his Silver and Blue livery R34 gave everyone around the world a taste of true Japanese performance.
From this day on the dream to own one of these legendary cars was cemented in countless fan's minds.
This car alone will go down as the Godfather of all JDM legends.
